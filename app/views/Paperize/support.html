How do I sign up?
How do I sign up?
You have two opportunities:
  1. You click on login and then on register to get to the registration form. After finishing the filling in, we will send you an e-mail to verify your e-mail address. You can edit your preferences by clicking on my subscription.
  2. You click on subscribe now on the landing page. Than, you can firstly edit your preferences before being forwarded to the registration form.

How do I change the preferences?
How do I change the preferences?
If you are logged in, you can change your preferences by clicking on my subscription.
